Project Summary

Project Overview

Developer: Nhambe DB

Tool: Microsoft Excel (Advanced Logic & Data Modeling)

Sector: Agribusiness / Quality Control

Executive Summary

Provides a standardized framework for Quality Control Clerks to assess maize batches at the point of intake, minimizing subjective decisions and reducing spoilage.

Problem Statement

Before this tool, there was no uniform method for calculating risk. Decisions were inconsistent, increasing mold contamination and lowering customer trust.

Solution & Technical Implementation

  • Weighted Risk Engine: Evaluates seven critical metrics including Moisture (%), Aflatoxin (ppb), and Insect Damage.
  • Automated Decision Logic: Accept/Reject logic triggers high-risk alerts for critical thresholds (e.g., moisture >14%).
  • Farmer Performance Tracking: Lookup system allows overview and comparisons of results across farms.

Key Impact

  • Waste Reduction: Blocks high-risk batches to lower probability of grain spoilage.
  • Data-Driven Extension: Provincial managers get clear data to address quality issues.
  • Operational Speed: Streamlines intake for faster throughput during peak harvest seasons.
