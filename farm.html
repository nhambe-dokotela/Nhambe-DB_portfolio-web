Project Overview

Developer: Nhambe DB

Tool: Microsoft Excel (Financial Modelling & VLOOKUPs)

Sector: Farm Management / Resource Optimization

Executive Summary

This dashboard is designed to help large-scale farm managers and cooperatives track the relationship between input costs and crop yields. By centralizing financial data, the tool helps identify “capital leaks” and ensures that the farm remains financially sustainable.

Problem Statement

Many farmers struggle to see the true profitability of individual crops because expenses (seeds, fertilizer, labour) are often tracked separately from revenue. Without a unified view, it is difficult to determine which crops are generating profit and which are operating at a loss.

Solution & Technical Implementation

  • Integrated Data Streams: Relational model linking Input Log (Expenses) with Yield Log (Revenue) via crop and date identifiers.
  • Variance Analysis: “Budgeted vs. Actual” tracking system to identify overspending in real-time.
  • Profit Margin Visualization: Charts ranking crops (e.g., Tomatoes vs. Cabbage) by actual Gross Profit Margin, not just tonnage.

Key Impact

  • Financial Sustainability: Helps managers identify capital leaks and improve the farm’s bottom line.
  • Strategic Planting: Insights guide future crop arrangements based on historical ROI.
  • Resource Management: Better allocation of labour and inputs across provinces and farm sites.
